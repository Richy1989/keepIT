keepIT is a fast, offline-first notes app that keeps your notes in sync across
all your devices in real time. It is the native Android client for the
open-source keepIT platform, which also has a web app and a REST API.

Note: keepIT is a client for a keepIT server. You host your own server (it is
free and open source) and sign in from the app with your server's address, so
your data stays entirely under your control.

Features:

* Plain notes and checklists, with an in-line Markdown editor
* Colors, pins, archive and trash to keep things organized
* Per-user lists to group notes your own way
* Reminders that fire natively via AlarmManager, even offline or with the app
  closed, and survive a reboot
* Full-text search across your notes
* Share notes with other users as Viewer or Editor, with the owner keeping
  control
* Real-time sync: a change on one device shows up on the others right away
* Offline-first: everything works without a connection: your edits are cached
  locally and a mutation outbox replays them automatically when you are back
  online
* Home-screen widget to jump straight into composing, a note, or your inbox
* Share text in from other apps to start a new note pre-filled

keepIT stores no analytics, contains no ads and no tracking. The refresh token
is kept in app-private storage and never leaves your device.
